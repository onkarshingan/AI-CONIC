How It Works

Simple for Professors,
Powerful for Students

Mark attendance in three ways — manual, face scan, or all-present shortcut.

1
Select Branch & Subject
Choose any of the 8 branches and their subjects — CSE, AI&ML, IT, BCA, MCA, MBA, BBA or Pharma. Subjects are grouped by branch for easy navigation.
2
Mark Present or Absent
Click Present / Absent per student card, or use "All Present" shortcut. Each card shows overall attendance and batch assignment.
3
Run Face Liveness Scan
Click the 🔬 button on any student card. Camera opens, Claude Vision captures 3 frames and verifies the student is physically present.
4
Auto-Alert Parents
If attendance falls below 75%, a parent alert is queued automatically. Go to the Alerts page and send with one click via Gmail.
5
Check Competition Rankings
Students are ranked by last 15 school days — overall and by class. Gold, silver, bronze podium motivates students to maintain attendance.

Face Scanner

Zero Proxy
Attendance

Claude Vision AI analyzes 3 separate frames and uses majority voting to confirm liveness — impossible to fool with any static image.

📸
3-Frame Capture
Camera takes 3 separate frames over 3 seconds for accurate multi-sample analysis.
🧠
Claude Vision Analysis
Each frame is sent to Claude Vision API. AI checks for depth, shadows, reflections, edges and texture.
🗳️
Majority Vote Verdict
2 out of 3 checks must confirm liveness. Prevents single-frame false positives.
🚫
Instant Spoof Block
If any single check detects a spoof with >62% confidence, the scan is immediately rejected and logged.
