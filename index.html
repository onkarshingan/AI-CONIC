How It Works

01
📸

Image Capture

Register each student by capturing 20 face images via webcam. Stored locally in the dataset folder.

02
🧠

Face Recognition

OpenCV + face_recognition encode and compare faces in real-time with sub-second latency.

03
📊

Excel Reports

Attendance auto-saved to attendance.xlsx with a sheet per course and date-stamped rows.

04
📧

Parent Alerts

Automatic SMTP email sent to parents when their child misses a class. Instant and reliable.
